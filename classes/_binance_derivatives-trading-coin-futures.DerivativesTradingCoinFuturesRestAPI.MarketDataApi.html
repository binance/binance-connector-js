          >,
      >

      Get compressed, aggregate trades. Market trades that fill in 100ms with the same price and the same taking side will have the quantity aggregated.

      support querying futures trade histories that are not older than one year If both startTime and endTime are sent, time between startTime and endTime must be less than 1 hour. If fromId, startTime, and endTime are not sent, the most recent aggregate trades will be returned. Only market trades will be aggregated and returned, which means the insurance fund trades and ADL trades won't be aggregated. Sending both startTime/endTime and fromId might cause response timeout, please send either fromId or startTime/endTime
