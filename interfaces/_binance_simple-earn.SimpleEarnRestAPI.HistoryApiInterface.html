      Get Collateral Record

      The time between startTime and endTime cannot be longer than 30 days. If startTime and endTime are both not sent, then the last 30 days' data will be returned. If startTime is sent but endTime is not sent, the next 30 days' data beginning from startTime will be returned. If endTime is sent but startTime is not sent, the 30 days' data before endTime will be returned.
